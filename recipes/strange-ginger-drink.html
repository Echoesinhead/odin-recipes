Strange ginger drink

Description

Ginger, dates, lemon. Strange combination? You don't say! Dew it. It's good

Easy AF, blend it add water and ice, rdy. FU

Ingredients

  • 1-2 cm of fresh ginger
  • 15 ml of lemon juice or just 1/5 of lemon if you want those juicy vitamins
  • 2 dates pitted
  • 250 ml of cold water
  • Ice optional, to taste
  1. Put peeled ginger, lemon or lemon juice and 2 pitted dates into the blender bowl.
  2. Add water
  3. Blend it well

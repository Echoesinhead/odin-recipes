choco-cake

Description

I’m not going to kid you, this makes a very rich, moist chocolate cake recipe. So if you are a faint of heart chocolate cake person, this is not the cake for you.

Based on the Old Fashioned Hershey’s recipe, I sort of went a bit heavy-handed with some changes testing cake after cake until I got it just the way I wanted it, but the end result was oh so worth it. The crumbs left on the plate gave that away.

Ingredients

  • 300g Flour
  • 3 eggs
  • 200ml milk
  • 100g Cocoa powder
  • 7g baking powder
  • 1 pinch of love
  • 1 pinch of salt
  • 2 pinches of cringe

Steps

  1. Preheat an oven to 350f
  2. Mix dry ingredients together in a bowl
  3. Mix wet ingredients together in another bowl
  4. Slowly add wet mixture to dry ingredients. Mix well.
  5. Pour the mixture into the baking pan
  6. Bake for 50 minutes until brown
  7. Check with the wooden toothpick. It must come out dry.
